About Us

IVY is made by 6 university students that matches all their interests. This perfume was made so that all 6 of them could use on a day-to-day basis. They wanted a perfume that suited both male and females. They decided for their perfumes to have a tropical scent. This idea came to the students as they were going over the seasons and realised that summer was the most popular season, creating a perfume that reminded them of being in a tropical environment.
